Mr Zwelibanzi Sapula

Chief Executive Officer

SADC Development Finance Resource Centre

Qualifications

  • Master of Business Administration – University of Cape Town
  • Bachelor of Engineering (BEng) in Chemical Engineering – University of Pretoria

Professional Background

With over 25 years of experience spanning financial services, civil engineering, venture capital, and private equity, Mr Zwelibanzi Sapula has held influential positions in several key institutions including the Development Bank of Southern Africa, the Presidential Infrastructure Coordinating Commission, and the National Empowerment Fund.

He has also contributed to nuclear energy initiatives through his work with the South African Nuclear Energy Corporation.

His career highlights include leading the transformation of Busamed Hospital Group into South Africa’s largest black-owned hospital group, spearheading the launch of a national Student Housing Program, and founding the National Water Partnership Office.

As Chair of the Sustainable Infrastructure Development Symposium South Africa, Sapula has played a pivotal role in shaping the country's broadband strategy, advancing projects in broadband expansion, satellite communications, and the digitization of national records.

Beyond his professional accomplishments, Sapula is passionate about mentoring and youth development. He is actively involved in children’s ministry at City Life Church, where he continues to inspire the next generation.
